Welcome to Card Brawl, a fun interactive card game where you get to battle Alvin, the blue floating demon guy over there.

To the left here, we can see you, the handsome little knight at the top.
Under you, we can see your health, mana,and shields. When your health reaches 0, you lose. Try not to let that happen.
You can use shield cards to gain shields which deflect incoming damage from Alvin.
You also have 3 mana, listed below your health. Each card has a mana cost. Use your mana wisely to combine cards for the best outcome.

To the right here we see Alvin at the top, his health right below him, and below that we see the card he's about to play (cards will be explained in the next section).
Finally, we can see the Play Cards and End Turn button.
Play Cards will play the card you've selected by clicking on it and highlighting it yellow. You can only select one card at a time.
End Turn will complete your turn, and give Alvin a chance to fight back with his selected card.
